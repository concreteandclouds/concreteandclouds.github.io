Welcome to **Concrete and Clouds**, a podcast hosted by Mark Beachill. 

Our goal is simple: discussing politics and society by bringing the real and the abstract together.

In each episode, we take a step back from the daily news cycle to examine the deeper structures, philosophies, and ideas that shape our world. From economics and the concept of the arbitrary, to deep-dives into modern literature like Alexander Douglas's *Against Identity*, we bridge the gap between abstract theory (the clouds) and the concrete reality of our daily lives.
